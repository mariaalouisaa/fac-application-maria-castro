ABOUT ME

I was born and raised in London by parents who migrated over in the pursuit of better opportunities, and for that I am so grateful. Not does London give so many options on what path you might take in life, but I have never been to any other city that is so diverse and accepting.

I spent the start of my adult life (ages 17-27) working as a chef, and although cooking still remains a passion of mine, it's an environment I fell out of love with. Lockdown last winter gave me the chnace to revaluate things.

After listening to a few webinars on coding my journey began, and it has been non-stop learning ever since. I use various free online platforms, such a FreeCodeCamp, CodeWars, Scrimble, Codcademy. They have all been great resources that have taught me a lot of fundamentals, but I would love the opportunity to join Founders and Coders for the chance to work closely with peers, tackle problems as a team and learn how a team of professional software developers work best.
